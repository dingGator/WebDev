Gainesville Nature and Parks

Gainesville has national Nature Parks as its backyard, Paynes Praries, Devils Millhopper, and many more . Deer running in its streets and yards is a major problem for Gainesville drivers and gardeners.
Gators are everywhere.

Devils Millhopper was formed by the rainwater became slightly acidic as it seeps thru the dead plants layers on the ground. The slightly acidic water then dissolved the underlying limestone bedrock forming a huge cavern. Eventually when there not enough limestone to support the weight of the land above the ceiling of the limestone collapsed forming the sinkhole.
