A Small City Delights With Big City Charms

A small beautiful city enjoyed by all. Home is where the heart is. Gainesville is the heart of Florida. It is a small city with big City charms.
Gainesville has national Nature Parks as its backyard, Paynes Praries, Devils Millhopper, and many more . Deer running in its streets and yards is a major problem for Gainesville drivers and gardeners.
Gators are everywhere.
Learning is the major industry of Gainesville with University of Florida (UF) and Santa Fe; UF is Gainesville; Gainesville is UF.
Sports is a part of life every weekend, football, swimming, basketball, volleyball, and many more.
UF's Shands place Gainesville on the map for innovation and patient care.

This website will help you enjoy Gainesville

It will highlight the nature, sports, and health of Gainesville. It will map out many must-see and not-to-be-missed parts of Gainesville
